Restaurant Description

Our quaint coffee shop and patisserie is located on scenic Forest Grove Run, which parallels some of the most splendid sights that Ardenweald has to offer. We are open early in the morning until noon, and have a variety of magnificent creations for you to try! Feel free to come by to pick up some of our signature glowberry macarons, our exquisite sylberry snowcakes, or our crisp and refreshing wintermelon sparkling water . We also have a wide variety of artisanal coffee products that regularly change with the season, so please come check us out today!
